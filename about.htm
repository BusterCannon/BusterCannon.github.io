About the Digger's Database

The Digger's Database is essentially a collection of various reviews that I've written on the gameplay aspects of the Megaman Legends series. I started posting these on Legends Station in the summer of 2008 after becoming inspired by another person's take on the different Kirby powers. I figured if it could work for Kirby, then why not Legends! I started doing the different special weapons, later moving on to bosses, reaverbots, Bonne machines, and even a few other elements from other Megaman games that I had taken a liking to.
